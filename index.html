Why 10% is Conservative

Based on specific findings from our CRO audit

  • 77.2% of sessions never reach a product page — Homepage has non-clickable banners, weak merchandising. Fixing navigation and category tiles alone can significantly lift PDP views.
  • 83.6% PDP→ATC drop-off — No reviews, no trust badges, no sticky ATC, poor CTA visibility. Industry benchmarks show 5–8% ATC rates vs Phab’s ~3.2%.
  • PageSpeed 37/100 mobile — Every 1s delay = ~7% conversion loss. Moving to Shapes theme with optimization targets 40+ mobile.
  • 8 of 13 GA4 events missing — Can’t optimize what you can’t measure. Full tracking setup alone improves targeting ROI.
  • Email & push near 0% — 1% email, 0.5% push. Setting up Klaviyo with 3 core flows (welcome, abandoned cart, post-purchase) adds a new revenue channel.
